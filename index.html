About Us

Serving Since 2014

Our Story

Way back 2014, Coffee Project opened its first branch at Starmall Alabang, Muntinlupa.

Serving good quality concoctions of espresso based drinks and mouth-watering food, Coffee Project aims to provide excellent coffee and gracious service creating memorable experience for people.

Our Vision

Our commitment is to deliver exceptional coffee through sustainable and ethical practices, changing the world one cup at a time.

We aspire to be the leading philanthropic coffee company, setting the highest standards for social and environmental impact. When you choose our coffee, you're choosing a better future for all.

Our Services

High Quality Customer Service

We pride ourselves on having a team of exceptionally friendly and highly skilled staff members. Each of our baristas is dedicated to providing you with a warm, welcoming atmosphere and a top-tier coffee experience.

Fresh Coffee Beans

The Coffee Project signature blend is composed of 70% Arabica coffee that is imported from Brazil and 30% Robusta coffee from Sumatra.

Best Quality Coffee
